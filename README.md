# EURUSD-market-data
EURUSD market data, 1 minute period, from 1999 to 2019.  
  
Columns format is    
``date;open price; high price; low price; close price``

date is formatted as "DDMMAAAA HHMMSS" where  
 - DD is day number  
 - MM is month number  
 - AAAA is the year  
 - HH is the hours  
 - MM is the minutes  
 - SS is the seconds  
 The time zone is Pacific Daylight Time (PDT).
